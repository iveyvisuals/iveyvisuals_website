Graphic Design

Graphic design is the backbone to creating an eye-catching and memorable brand. Whether it’s for an event, a campaign, or everyday marketing, each design is crafted to feel cohesive, intentional, and uniquely you.

Merchandise Design

Designed for Western Ontario Organization of Filipinos

“Pamilya,” the Tagalog word for “family,” was used as the central theme for this merchandise design. Filipino culture is deeply rooted in care, connection, and hospitality. These values are reflected in the organization’s community and spirit. Incorporating “pamilya” into the design created an opportunity to celebrate and proudly wear Filipino identity in a meaningful and unified way.

Event Design

Designed for an 18th Birthday Party

Have a milestone coming up? Celebrate in style with event graphics that feel cohesive and thoughtful from start to finish. From custom invitations to table cards and food labels, each piece is designed to reflect the tone of your event and leave a lasting impression on your guests.

Menu Design

Designed for Sweet & Sassie Bakeshop

Your products deserve to be presented in a way that is both beautiful and easy to navigate. For Sweet & Sassie Bakeshop, a custom menu board was designed to reflect the charm and personality of their brand. This menu was created specifically for their new bakery on wheels, combining playful visuals with clean organization to help customers browse with ease. Every detail was crafted to enhance the overall experience and bring their dessert trailer to life.
