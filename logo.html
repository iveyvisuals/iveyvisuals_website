Logo Design

Your logo is the heart of your visual identity. It’s what shows up on your packaging, website, social media, and more. It should feel unmistakably you. Let's design a logo that capture your brand’s personality in one clear, lasting impression.

Monochrome Text-Based Logos

Designed for Sycuts Studio

This logo takes on a clean, monochrome look with a focus on bold text and intentional simplicity. The design merges minimalism with strong visual impact to clearly represent Sycuts’ identity as a modern barbershop. The custom letterform and stylized 'S' convey both professionalism and edge, making it easily recognizable across digital and print platforms.

Multicolour Text-Based Logos

Designed for IveyVisuals Design Co.

This multicolour text-based logo was created to embody the studio’s identity with clarity and intention. The use of varying typographic weights and a bold yet elegant colour palette reflects the brand’s multidimensional approach to design. Each letterform and tone was carefully selected to communicate both professionalism and creativity, while remaining versatile across print, digital, and motion formats.

Image Based Logos

Designed for Kenzo Ramen

This conceptual rebrand explores a refreshed visual identity for Kenzo Ramen, with a focus on developing an image-based logo that blends tradition and clarity. Created as a personal design project, this rebrand uses visual symbolism—specifically a refined illustration of the original character icon—as the foundation of the logo. Rather than relying solely on typography, the logo integrates a memorable graphic element to reflect the brand’s personality and origin.

Note: This project was done independently and is not affiliated with Kenzo Ramen.
