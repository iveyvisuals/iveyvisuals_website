Brand Design

A strong brand starts with a bold first impression. From logos to style guides, every detail should reflect who you are and what you stand for. Let's design a brand that speak for themselves — clear, confident, and ready to be remembered.

Custom Presentations

Designed for Enhanced Athletics

A series of workout programs for Enhanced Athletics was transformed into a visually engaging and easy-to-follow presentation. The goal was to make the content clear, motivating, and accessible for clients of all fitness levels. Design choices focused on clean layouts, bold headings, and purposeful visuals to help simplify complex routines and enhance the learning experience. The final product reflects the brand’s commitment to excellence and provides clients with an organized, professional tool to guide their progress.

Social Media Branding

Designed for Western Ontario Organization of Filipinos

When someone lands on your page, the visual identity sets the tone. A cohesive and engaging feed encourages visitors to keep scrolling, stay longer, and connect with your content. This Instagram brand theme was created for WOOF UWO, using traditional Filipino colors and cultural motifs to reflect the spirit of the club. The design aimed to capture not only WOOF’s vibrant community, but also its deep roots in heritage and unity. From highlight covers to post graphics, every visual was curated to feel bold, expressive, and uniquely WOOF.

Brand Refresh

Designed for Kenzo Ramen

This rebrand was pursued as a self-initiated project to practice and expand branding skills. Inspired by the visual identity of the well-known ramen house, this concept aimed to give Kenzo Ramen a fresh, modern look while staying rooted in its cultural origins. Research into Japanese design principles and traditional aesthetics informed the color palette, typography, and visual motifs. Key elements from the original branding were thoughtfully retained and reimagined to ensure a sense of familiarity and brand continuity.

Note: This project was done independently and is not affiliated with Kenzo Ramen.
