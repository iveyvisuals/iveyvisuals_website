Brand Design

Making a brand is all about making a statement at first glance. IveyVisuals aims to design brands that speak for themselves and leave the greatest first impression upon your clients.

Kenzo Ramen Rebrand

Pursued as a personal project to enhance skills, a Kenzo ramen rebrand was done to create a fresh look on the famous ramen house. Research was done on Japanese culture to ensure that the brand remains authentic to its roots. In addition, effort was made to maintain old elements of the logo and use it in the new logo. This is done through the "guy" found in the Kenzo logo.
